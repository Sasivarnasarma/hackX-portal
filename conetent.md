### hackX 11.0 (University Tier)
- **Title**: Registration Guidelines
**Text** :
Ensure all required information is available before starting your registration.
Provide accurate and up-to-date details for your team, including:
- Team Leader’s contact details
- University or higher education institution
- Details of all team members
